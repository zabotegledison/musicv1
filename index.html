Block size is fixed at 2 bars: 4 fragments. Opening/ending forms replace one bar inside the pattern phrase.
Loops are prepared at a fixed original tempo and time-stretched to match Study BPM without changing pitch. If no track is selected, a simple metronome plays instead.
